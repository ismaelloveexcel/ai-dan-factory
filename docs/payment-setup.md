# How to Accept Your First Payment — No Excuses

> You can have a working payment link in **under 30 minutes**. This guide shows you how.

---

## Option 1: LemonSqueezy (Recommended)

**Why:** No business registration required. Works in 140+ countries. Handles VAT/tax automatically. You get paid to your bank or PayPal.

### Steps:

1. **Go to** [lemonsqueezy.com](https://www.lemonsqueezy.com) and create a free account
2. **Add your payout method** (PayPal or bank transfer) — Settings → Payouts
3. **Create a Store** — give it any name (your product name is fine)
4. **Create a Product:**
   - Click "Products" → "New Product"
   - Name: e.g. "GameForge Birthday Card — $7"
   - Type: "Single Payment"
   - Price: set your price
   - Click "Publish"
5. **Copy your checkout link** — it looks like `https://yourstore.lemonsqueezy.com/buy/xxxxx`
6. **Paste it anywhere** — your website, a tweet, a WhatsApp message, a Reddit post

That's it. When someone pays, money goes straight to your account (minus ~5% + Stripe fees).

### To add it to your launch kit:
Paste the LemonSqueezy link as your main CTA button URL. No code needed.

## Option 2: Gumroad (Even Simpler for Digital Products)

**Why:** The simplest possible setup. Great for e-books, templates, and one-off digital products.

### Steps:

1. **Go to** [gumroad.com](https://gumroad.com) and sign up free
2. **Click "New Product"** → set a name and price
3. **Add a description and cover image** (optional but helps)
4. **Publish** → copy your product link
5. **Share the link** — you're done

Gumroad takes 10% on the free plan. You can upgrade to $10/month to reduce fees to ~3%.
